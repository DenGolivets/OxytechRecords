ABOUT US

Oxytech Records - is a developing Ukrainian label that created by two producers Terra4Beat (Den Golivets) & Neurofaza (Sasha Scebro) in July 2013, which is specialized in the production of high-quality music and Techno style is different from many of its underground and individual sound. The purpose of creation is promotion of young talents and has already experienced in the largest electronic stores worldwide. We work with stores like Beatport, Junodownload, iTunes, TraxSource, Spotify, Shazam & etc. Distribution from Proton SoundSystem. The style of the label: Underground Techno & Dark Techno, Hard Techno.

Oxytech Limited (sub-label October 2015). The style of the label: Techno, Minimal House & Techno, Tech House, etc.

Oxytech Planet (sub-label June 2020). The style of the label: Hard Techno, Underground Techno, Techno, Minimal House & Techno, Tech House, etc.
